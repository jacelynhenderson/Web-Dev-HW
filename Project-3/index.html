INTRODUCTION

Weslandia is a fantastic childrens book that tells a story about a young boy named Wesley. This book makes the reader believe anything is possible and that being unique is not weird, but awesome. The pictures in the book are also very exciting to look at and and makes the book come to life . This websites purpose is to go in depth about this books personailty. Enjoy learning more about Weslandia



This fantastical picture book, like its hero, is bursting at the seams with creativity. . . . a vigorous shot in the arm to nonconformists everywhere - PUBLISHERS WEEKLY

This was one of my FAVORITE picture books in elementary school (about 12 years ago). It obviously left an impression because I still remember it all these years later, and I just bought it for a child I know. Weslandia is far more creative than many picture books, and it is also educational. Kids will learn a lot about aspects of civilizations and how they develop. I hope this book stays in print for many years to come so that I can read it to my children. This book may be challenging for younger readers. I would recommend it for 8 year-olds and up. - Goofygirl13, Customer Review
